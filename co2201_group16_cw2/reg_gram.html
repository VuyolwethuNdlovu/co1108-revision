Regular Grammar

Brief Description of Regular Grammars: 

A regular grammar is just another way to represent regular
language. A grammar defines your language.

There is a general structure of how to represent regular
grammars as seen below:

<NonTerm>::= Term <NonTerm>|
                           Term <NonTerm>|
                           …|                          
                           λ





Key point from the tutorial: 

 Non-Terminals can be seen as States and Terminals can be seen as transitions
